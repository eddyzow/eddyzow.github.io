CHEATING IS NOT COOL!

We noticed this is your first time playing JSBeats on a new browser! Welcome!

Games like JSBeats are very prone to hacking, especially if access to the console is available.

Although an anti-cheat is already implemented and measures have been taken to make sure cheating doesn't happen, please do not cheat.

If you see a cheater or think there may be one in your game, please report them and mods will watch their replay.

JSBeats does not tolerate cheating. If you are caught cheating, you will be banned immediately with no warning.

You could be suspended by JSBeats if you have a couple of very good runs. If that happens, shoot an email to thegameshowlobby@gmail.com along with a link to a video of you playing (keyboard cam) and we'll take care of the rest.
