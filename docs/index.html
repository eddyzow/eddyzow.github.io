What's this site, and who runs it?

Hello! I'm Eddy, a high schooler from Massachusetts and the developer of everything within this website! I'm particularly interested in the fields of astronomy and astrophysics, as well as robotics. My hobbies include writing, editing YouTube videos, and Science Olympiad. I am also an avid Dunkin' enjoyer.

As a developer I am still learning. However, I hope the projects I make will get way better over time. You can try some of them out by clicking the Projects tab above!

This site was built using raw HTML/CSS/JS, open-sourced on my GitHub page. The space-themed background was made possible by particles.js. Icons by Font Awesome.
