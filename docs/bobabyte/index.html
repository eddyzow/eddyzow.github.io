ready for a bobatastic adventure of creativity?

bobabyte is an up-and-coming all-day coding event for middle and high schoolers, to be held at TBD on TBD. Scratch, Java, HTML - if you can show it off, you can build it at BobaByte! It's absolutely free to attend, merch and food are free, and the coolest projects will win awesome prizes. And let's not forget arguably the best part - free boba will be served!

Is BobaByte a hackathon?

Both yes and no! A traditional hackathon is often targeted towards experienced coding teams and might last up to a few days long. We built BobaByte as a way to let new coders express their creativity - and win prizes along the way!
