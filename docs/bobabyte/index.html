ready for a bobatastic adventure of creativity?

BobaByte is an up-and-coming all-day coding event for middle and high schoolers, to be held at TBD on October 26, 2024. Scratch, Java, HTML - everyone from newbies to experienced coders are invited! It's absolutely free to attend. Projects will be judged on many categories, so everyone has a chance to win awesome prizes. And let's not forget arguably the best part - free food, merch, and boba will be served!

Is BobaByte a hackathon?

Both yes and no! A traditional hackathon is often targeted towards experienced coding teams and might last up to a few days long. We built BobaByte as a way to let new coders express their creativity - and win prizes along the way!
