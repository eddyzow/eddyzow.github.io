BobaByte

a social coding event where you make literally anything you want, alongside free merch, food, bubble tea, and prizes.

october 26, 2024, 9am - 6pm. location TBD.

Ready for a bobatastic adventure of creativity?

BobaByte is an up-and-coming all-day social coding event for middle and high schoolers, to be held at TBD on October 26, 2024. Scratch, Java, HTML - everyone from newbies to experienced coders are invited! It's absolutely free to attend. Projects will be judged on many categories, so everyone has a chance to win awesome prizes. And let's not forget arguably the best part - free food, merch, and boba will be served!

A classic hackathon experience with a twist.

We're hoping to see people who have never attended a social coding event (i.e. hackathon) before come to BobaByte. BobaByte will be like a classic hackathon where projects are craeted and judged, but we also want to emphasize the learning experience! We'll be offering workshops on Scratch apps, Java apps, CAD design, and HTML. There's plenty to learn for everyone!

A reputable team.

BobaByte is led by a team of high school seniors with national-level awards in STEM and robotics. We're taking care of all the logistics - you just have to show up and have fun!

Everything's free.

Food, merch, and boba drops during the day are all fully covered by our amazing sponsors, who have made BobaByte possible. You can even get reimbursement for travel to BobaByte!

Amazing prizes.

We'll be judging based on categories from Coolest Project and Most Useful to Judges' Choice and more. There's a category for everyone! The winners will receive anything from gift cards and goodies to super cool robotics kits!
