How to Play Passcode

Ã—
  • You have six tries to guess the six-digit passcode.
  • For each guess, if a tile turns green, the digit is correct and in the correct spot.
  • If a tile turns yellow, the digit is in the passcode, but not in the correct spot.
  • If a tile turns black, the digit is not in the passcode.
  • With each guess, you'll also get the sum of differences of digits from the passcode. This will tell you how far away you are from the passcode.
Can you crack the passcode? A new puzzle is released at midnight Eastern time every day.
