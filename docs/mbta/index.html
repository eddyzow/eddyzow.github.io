WHERE IS MY COMMUTER RAIL TRAIN?

This tool serves to locate the next MBTA Commuter Rail trains to the station closest to your location and estimates the distance and predicted arrival time of such trains.

Created by eddyzow (eddyzow.github.io) in June 2024 as a precursor to a huge project I will be starting in July!

Credits to MBTA for their V3 API, which I used to obtain train data for this project!

Please note that this webapp collects user-consented geolocation data to determine the nearest MBTA station to you. If you do not consent to the use of such data, do not use this app.
