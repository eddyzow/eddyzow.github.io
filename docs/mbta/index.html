Where Is My Commuter Rail Train?

This tool serves to locate the next MBTA Commuter Rail trains to the station closest to your location and estimates the distance and time of such trains.

Trying to locate your nearest MBTA Station...

Updating in 20 seconds...

Please note that this webapp collects IP geolocation data as well as user-consented geolocation data to determine the nearest MBTA station to you. If you do not consent to the use of such data, do not use this app.

May be misleading because time to arrival will be increasing for trains traveling away from you.

Currently does not support stations that serve more than one line of the Commuter Rail (including North and South Stations). I will be working on this.
